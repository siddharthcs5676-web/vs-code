Playstation 5

The PlayStation 5 (PS5) is a high-performance home gaming console from Sony, featuring an ultra-high-speed SSD for near-instant loading, 4K resolution support, and up to 120fps capability. Released in 2020, the lineup now includes the compact "Slim" model (1TB) and the powerful PS5 Pro, which features advanced ray tracing and improved, AI-powered upscaling.
